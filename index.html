🔐 Google Authenticator to TOTP

💡 使用说明：
1. 在 Google Authenticator 点击“导出账号”，截取那个大型二维码。
2. 点击下方按钮上传截图，网页会解析出所有账号。
3. 可以直接查看验证码，或者用其他支持 TOTP 的应用扫描二维码导入。
💡 How to use:
1. In Google Authenticator, tap "Export accounts" and take a screenshot of the large QR code.
2. Click the button below to upload the screenshot, and the page will parse all your accounts.
3. You can view the codes directly, or scan the QR codes with any other TOTP app to import.
🔒 安全说明：所有解析仅在您的浏览器本地完成，不会有任何联网操作，您的数据绝对安全，请放心使用！
🔒 Security Note: All parsing happens locally in your browser, no network requests are made. Your data is completely safe and private!
